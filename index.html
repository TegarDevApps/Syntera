Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions and make the most of Maps Explore’s key features, from traffic updates to emergency alerts

Read more FAQ ->

How do I get real-time traffic updates?

You can use live traffic applications such as Google Maps or Waze to get up-to-date information on the traffic conditions in your area.

How do I report an issue in the city?

To report an issue, you can use local government apps, hotlines, or websites dedicated to citizen services. Many cities have platforms for reporting problems like broken infrastructure or public disturbances.

How can I plan my journey through the city?

Journey planning can be done using map services like Google Maps, Apple Maps, or dedicated public transportation apps that help you navigate routes, schedules, and transportation options.

How will I be notified of emergencies or disasters?

Emergency notifications are usually sent through national alert systems, push notifications from weather or government apps, or SMS messages from service providers. You can also subscribe to emergency alert services in your area.
